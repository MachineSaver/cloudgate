The LoRa Basics Station protocol simplifies management of large scale LoRaWAN networks. LoRa Basics Station is the preferred way of connecting Gateways to most LoRaWAN Network Servers.

This section contains information on how to get the Basics Station application on your CloudGate and how to connect your CloudGate to some LoRaWAN Network Providers.

 

LoRa Basics Station website: https://doc.sm.tc/station/

 

Install Basics Station on your CloudGate
 

BasicStation binaries are available on the ftp server: ftp://ftp.option.com/basicstation
