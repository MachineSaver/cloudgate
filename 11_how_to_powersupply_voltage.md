How to retrieve the power supply voltage?
To retrieve the power supply voltage use the next command:

cat /sys/class/main/cortex/supply_voltage

 

Requirements:

Minimum firmare version : 2.62.0
Supported hardware: CloudGate LTE WW, CloudGate LTE Rev 3, CloudGate LTE WW Rev4, CloudGate mini, CloudGate micro and CloudGate nano
